TURMERIC

Scientific Name:Curcuma longa
Habitat:Native to South Asia, especially India, thrives in tropical and subtropical regions.
Medicinal Values:
- Contains curcumin, a powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant compound.
- Supports joint health and is used to alleviate symptoms of arthritis.
- Enhances digestion and helps manage digestive disorders.
Climate:It can be grown in diverse tropical conditions from sea level to 1500 m above sea level, at a temperature range of 20°C-35°C with an annual rainfall of 1500 mm or more, under rainfed or irrigated conditions.
Soil:Turmeric can grow in a variety of soils, but it does best in well-drained, sandy or clay loam soils that are rich in organic matter
