PEPPERMINT

Scientific Name:Mentha × piperita
Habitat:Native to Europe and the Middle East, commonly found in temperate regions.
Medicinal Values:
- Relieves digestive issues such as bloating, gas, and indigestion.
- Provides relief from headaches and migraines due to its cooling effect.
- Possesses antimicrobial properties and is used to freshen breath.
Climate:grows best in temperatures between 25°C–30°C. This range is ideal because it's cool enough to avoid heat stress and warm enough to prevent frostbite.The best climates for the average peppermint variety are those with long warm days and cool nights.
Soil:It prefers well-draining, loose, and organically rich soil.
