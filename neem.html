NEEM

Scientific Name:Azadirachta indica
Habitat:Native to India, also found in tropical and subtropical regions.
Medicinal Values:
- Used in traditional medicine for its antifungal, antibacterial, and anti-inflammatory properties.
- Helps treat skin disorders, oral health issues, and acts as a natural pesticide.
- Supports detoxification and improves liver health.
Climate:Neem trees can grow in a wide range of climates, but they do best in tropical and subtropical regions.Can tolerate high summer temperatures up to 50°C, but they cannot tolerate frost or temperatures below 4°C.
Soil:It grows well on almost all kinds of soils which are well drained such as loamy, clayey and black cotton soil.
