ALOE VERA

Scientific Name:Aloe vera
Habitat: Native to the Arabian Peninsula, but widely cultivated in tropical and arid regions.
Medicinal Values:
-Contains anti-inflammatory and soothing properties for skin issues like burns and wounds.
- Aids in digestion and acts as a natural laxative.
- Supports the immune system and has antioxidant effects.
Climate:It prefers temperatures between 55°C and 85°C.Also they need to be housed in a bright location with some direct sun in winter months
Soil:It grows best in well-draining soil that's aerated and provides nutrients.
