TULSI

Scientific Name:Ocimum tenuiflorum
Habitat: Native to India, commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions.
Medicinal Values:
- Acts as an adaptogen, helping the body manage stress.
- Possesses antibacterial, antiviral, and antifungal properties.
- Used to treat respiratory issues, digestive disorders, and skin condition.
Climate:It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring warm temperatures and plenty of sunlight.
Soil: It grows best in well-drained, fertile soil.
