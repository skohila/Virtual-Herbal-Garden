ASHWAGANDHA(Indian Ginseng)

Scientific Name:Withania somnifera
Habitat:Native to India, thrives in dry regions and cultivated in the Middle East and Africa.
Medicinal Values:
- Relieves digestive issues such as bloating, gas, and indigestion.
- Provides relief from headaches and migraines due to its cooling effect.
- Possesses antimicrobial properties and is used to freshen breath.
Climate:It prefers a sub-tropical climatical areas receiving 500-750 mm rainfall are suitable for cultivation. The crop requires dry season during the growing period. Temperature between 20°C-35°C is most suitable for its cultivation.
Soil:Grows well in sandy loam soil, in slightly alkaline soil with pH of 7.5 to 8.0. It grows better in 600-1200 m altitude.
